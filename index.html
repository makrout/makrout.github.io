Research Associate @ University of Manitoba


My research interests lie broadly in optimization, statistical signal processing, artificial intelligence for dermatology and antenna theory for wireless communication.


My current work is focused around:
  • scaling beyond 5G (B5G) communication systems in terms of the number of compact antennas and the operational bandwidth, thereby reducing the cost/power and environmental footprints of future communication systems,
  • deep learning for healthcare by implementing, deploying, and clinically validating AI models for diagnostic screening in hospitals. This is a major step forward AI-driven medecine fulfilling its promise of improving triaging.
